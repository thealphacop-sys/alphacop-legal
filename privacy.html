Privacy Policy for Alpha Cop

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

Alpha Cop LLC ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") operates the Alpha Cop mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Alpha Cop does not directly collect, store, transmit, or process personal information from users.

The only user-related data involved in the operation of the App is processed exclusively by Apple through its App Store and StoreKit systems, including:

  • Apple ID account authentication
  • Auto-renewable subscription management
  • Billing and payment processing
  • App Store reviews voluntarily submitted by users

Alpha Cop LLC does not have access to your Apple ID credentials, payment information, or subscription billing details.

2. Subscriptions

All subscription purchases, renewals, cancellations, and billing matters are handled directly by Apple. Subscriptions may be managed in your device settings under:

Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

We do not store payment information or process payments directly.

3. Analytics and Tracking

Alpha Cop does not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or cookies.

4. App Store Reviews

If you submit a review through the App Store, that information is publicly displayed and managed by Apple. We do not control or modify App Store review data.

5. Children's Privacy

Alpha Cop is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

Because Alpha Cop does not collect or store user data on its own servers, we rely on Apple’s secure infrastructure for authentication and payment processing.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the “Last Updated” date above.
